Testovanie zvislost

Budeme testova prpadn korelciu medzi rznymi dvojicami hodnt v naich dtach pomocou linernej regresie. Vyuijeme na to vstavan funckiu lm().

1. Hypotza: Korelcia medzi Vekom a BMI

age_bmi_lm = lm(age~bmi, data)
plot(data$age, data$bmi, pch = 16, col = "blue", xlab = "Vek", ylab = "BMI")
abline(age_bmi_lm, col = "red")

summary(age_bmi_lm)
